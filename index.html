About NLC India Limited

NLC India Limited (formerly Neyveli Lignite Corporation), a Navratna CPSU under the Ministry of Coal, Government of India, has been at the forefront of lignite mining, thermal and renewable energy since 1956.

Vision

To emerge as a leading energy company with global standards and social responsiveness.

Mission

To sustain expertise in power & mining sectors with focus on growth, innovation, and sustainability.

Values

Environmental stewardship, safety, innovation, and community development.

Key Projects & Achievements

Neyveli Thermal Power

Commissioned Asia’s largest lignite-fired boiler with 2 × 500 MW units.

Renewable Energy

Expanding solar & wind energy footprint across Tamil Nadu & beyond.

Lignite Mining

Over 30 million tonnes of lignite production annually from Neyveli & Rajasthan.

Sustainability & CSR

NLC India Limited is committed to a sustainable future through green energy, ecological restoration, and impactful CSR initiatives. Here are some of the key highlights:

Corporate Plan 2030 & Vision 2047

Expanding renewable capacity from 1.43 GW to 10.11 GW by 2030 and aiming for 77% renewables by 2047 with a Net-Zero goal by 2070.

Awards & Recognition

Winner of GEEF Global Environment Award 2024 and Greentech Environment Award 2025 for excellence in mining reclamation, pollution control, and green initiatives.

Eco-Friendly Campaigns

Installed solar trees and mini windmills from scrap, developed Podhigai Herbal Park, and reclaimed 8 hectares into a lush green Lignite Park.

Renewable Push

Commissioned multiple solar PV plants (141 MW) and a 51 MW wind farm. Fast-tracking RE growth with ₹7,000 Cr investment.
